# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales performance, covering key metrics such as sales, profit, quantity, delivery status, customer segments, and product category profitability. It helps in understanding business performance and identifying actionable insights.

## 🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance
Understand customer segment behavior
Evaluate product category profitability
Track delivery performance and efficiency
Identify top-performing products and regions

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
Total Sales
Total Profit
Total Quantity Sold
Profit Margin %
Average Discount
On-Time Delivery Rate
Late Delivery Rate

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
👥 Customer Segment Analysis
Interactive slicer for Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office
Segment-wise comparison of sales and profit
📦 Category Profitability
Analysis of profit across product categories
Helps identify high and low-margin categories
🚚 Delivery Performance
Breakdown of on-time, late, and cancelled deliveries
Shipment delay analysis
📍 Regional Insights
Sales and profit distribution by region and state

---

## 📅 Trend Analysis
Monthly sales and profit trends
Seasonal performance tracking

---

## 🏆 Product Insights
Top-performing products by sales and profit
Order quantity distribution
